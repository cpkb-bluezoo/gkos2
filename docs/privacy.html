Privacy Policy

GKOS Keyboard for Android

This privacy policy describes how GKOS Keyboard ("the app") handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

GKOS Keyboard does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely offline and makes no network connections.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device to improve your typing experience:

  • User dictionary — words you type that are not in the built-in dictionary are saved locally so they can be suggested in future.
  • Word pair frequencies — the app learns which words you commonly type together to provide better contextual suggestions.
  • Layout preferences — your selected language and layout variant settings.

All of this data is stored in the app's private storage on your device. It is never transmitted to any server, third party, or external service.

Keystroke Privacy

As an input method (keyboard), the app processes your keystrokes to produce text. Keystroke data is used only to generate the characters you type and to update the local user dictionary and word suggestions. Keystrokes are never logged, recorded, or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users. No personal information is collected from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
